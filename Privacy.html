Our Privacy Statement

We collect personal information from you, including your name, contact details and location information.

We collect your personal details in order to ensure bookings made via our online web application are secure. We collect your location information in order to ensure directions via Google Maps are accurate.

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter your personal information, we will be unable to secure bookings made online. If you choose not to enter your location information, we will be unable to provide accurate directions to the establishment.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you would like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at info@thehaukai.com, or (09) 401-4019, or 1 Island Street Kerikeri, Bay of Islands.
